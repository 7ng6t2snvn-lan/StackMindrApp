Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 15, 2026

StackMindr ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy describes what data we collect, how we collect it, how we use it, and who we share it with when you use the StackMindr application ("the App").

Data We Collect

When you use StackMindr, we collect the following categories of data:

  • Account information: Email address, display name, and authentication credentials when you create an account via email, Google Sign-In, or Apple Sign-In.
  • Protocol data: Compound names, dosing schedules, frequencies, time slots, and related tracking information that you enter into the App.
  • Inventory data: Information about your compound inventory, including vial sizes, remaining amounts, and related details.
  • Log data: Records of when you mark doses as taken or skipped.
  • AI conversation data: If you use the AI feature, the text of your questions and conversations with the AI assistant.

How We Collect Data

All data is collected directly from you through your interactions with the App. We do not collect data from third-party sources. Authentication is handled by Google Firebase Authentication. All user-entered data (schedules, inventory, logs) is stored in Google Cloud Firestore, associated with your authenticated user account.

How We Use Your Data

We use your data solely to provide and improve the StackMindr App:

  • Account data is used to authenticate you and sync your data across devices.
  • Protocol, inventory, and log data is used to display your schedules, calculate dosing, track adherence, and power app features such as the calculator and stock tracker.
  • AI conversation data is used to generate personalized AI responses about your protocol. See the "Third-Party AI Service" section below for details.

Third-Party AI Service

StackMindr includes an optional AI assistant feature. When you use this feature, the following data is shared with a third-party AI service:

  • What data is sent: Your compound names, doses, schedules, and the text of your questions are sent to generate a personalized response. No account credentials or email addresses are included in AI requests.
  • Who the data is sent to: AI requests are processed by Anthropic, PBC (San Francisco, CA), the maker of Claude AI, via their API. Requests are routed through a Cloudflare Worker proxy.
  • How Anthropic handles the data: Data sent to Anthropic via the API is processed solely to generate a response. Anthropic does not use API data to train its AI models. Anthropic's data handling is governed by their Privacy Policy and API Data Usage Policy, which provide protections consistent with this Privacy Policy.
  • Your consent is required: The AI feature is entirely optional. Before any data is sent to Anthropic, you must explicitly consent to the data sharing through an in-app consent screen. You may revoke this consent at any time in the App's Settings under Privacy. If you decline or revoke consent, no data is sent to Anthropic and all other App features remain fully functional.

Third-Party Services

In addition to Anthropic (described above), StackMindr uses the following third-party services:

  • Google Firebase Authentication: Manages user accounts and sign-in. Governed by Google's Privacy Policy.
  • Google Cloud Firestore: Stores your protocol data securely in the cloud. Data is associated with your authenticated user ID and protected by security rules that prevent access by other users.
  • Cloudflare: Provides DNS and proxies AI requests. Governed by Cloudflare's Privacy Policy.

Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored in Google Cloud Firestore with per-user security rules. Only you can access your data when authenticated. We use industry-standard security measures including HTTPS encryption for all data in transit and Firebase security rules to protect data at rest.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties for marketing or advertising purposes. Data is only shared with the third-party services listed above (Firebase, Firestore, Cloudflare, and optionally Anthropic) as necessary to operate the App.

Data Retention and Deletion

Your data is retained as long as you maintain an active account. You may export all of your data at any time using the Export feature in Settings. You may delete all of your data using the "Clear All Data" option in Settings. To permanently delete your account and all associated data, contact us at the email below.

Children's Privacy

StackMindr is not intended for use by anyone under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect information from minors.

Medical Disclaimer

StackMindr is a personal tracking and educational tool. The information provided in the App, including the Glossary and AI responses, is for educational purposes only and does not constitute medical advice. Dosing ranges and compound information are based on published research (cited with PubMed links where available) and community protocols. Always consult a qualified healthcare provider before starting, modifying, or stopping any health-related protocol.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any significant changes by updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
