Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 18, 2026

Overview

StackMindr ("we," "our," or "the app") is a peptide and hormone protocol tracking application. We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about how your data is handled.

Data We Collect

When you create an account and use StackMindr, we collect and store the following data in Firebase (Google Cloud):

  • Account information (email address, display name)
  • Protocol schedules (compound names, doses, frequencies, timing)
  • Inventory data (vial details, stock levels, vendor info)
  • Dose logs (dates and times of administered doses)
  • Subscription status (Pro tier, transaction IDs)

AI Features

If you enable the AI Protocol Assistant (Pro feature), your protocol data — including compound names, doses, schedules, and your questions — is sent to Anthropic (the maker of Claude AI) to generate personalized responses. Anthropic processes your data solely to generate a response. Data sent via their API is not used to train AI models and is handled in accordance with Anthropic's Privacy Policy.

AI features require explicit consent before activation. You can revoke AI consent at any time in Settings, which immediately stops data sharing with Anthropic.

How We Use Your Data

Your data is used solely to provide the app's functionality — tracking your protocols, calculating doses, managing inventory, and powering the AI assistant. We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties for marketing purposes.

Data Storage & Security

All data is stored securely in Firebase (Google Cloud Platform) and is protected by Firebase Authentication. Only you can access your data when signed in to your account. Data is transmitted using industry-standard encryption (HTTPS/TLS).

Third-Party Services

StackMindr uses the following third-party services:

  • Firebase Authentication — for account creation and sign-in (email/password, Google, Apple)
  • Cloud Firestore — for secure data storage
  • Anthropic API — for AI Protocol Assistant responses (Pro only, requires consent)
  • Apple StoreKit — for subscription purchases and management

Data Retention & Deletion

Your data is retained as long as your account is active. You can delete your account and all associated data at any time from the Settings tab within the app. Account deletion permanently removes all schedules, inventory, logs, and user data from our servers.

Children's Privacy

StackMindr is not intended for use by children under the age of 17. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Subscriptions

StackMindr offers an optional auto-renewable subscription (StackMindr Pro) at $4.99/month. Payment is charged to your Apple Account on confirmation. The subscription auto-renews unless canceled at least 24 hours before the end of the current period. You can manage or cancel your subscription in your iOS Settings.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy or your data, contact us at stackmindr@proton.me.
