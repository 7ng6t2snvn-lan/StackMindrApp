Delete Your Account

Last updated: May 12, 2026

Overview

StackMindr ("we," "our," or "the app") lets you permanently delete your account and all associated personal data at any time. This page explains exactly how to do it and what gets removed.

In-App Account Deletion

The fastest way to delete your account is from inside the app:

  1. Open StackMindr on your device.
  2. Sign in to your account if you're not already signed in.
  3. Tap the Settings tab.
  4. Scroll down to the Account section.
  5. Tap Delete Account.
  6. Read the confirmation screen and tap Confirm Delete to proceed.
  7. Re-enter your password to verify the request.

The deletion is immediate. Your account is closed and your data is removed from our active systems on confirmation.

Delete by Email Request

If you can no longer access the app — for example, you've uninstalled it, lost access to your device, or are unable to sign in — you can request account deletion by emailing us:

Email: justin@stackmindr.app

Subject: Account Deletion Request

Include: The email address associated with your StackMindr account.

We will verify your identity by emailing the address on file and complete the deletion within 7 business days of confirmation.

What Gets Deleted

The following data is permanently removed when your account is deleted:

  • Your account credentials (email address, password hash, display name)
  • All protocol schedules (compound names, doses, frequencies, timing)
  • All inventory data (vial details, stock levels, vendor information)
  • All dose logs (dates, times, and history of administered doses)
  • All lab work entries and uploaded laboratory PDFs
  • All uploaded photos (inventory photos, lab document images)
  • All notes, body composition entries, and tracked metrics
  • App settings and personal preferences

What May Be Retained

For legal and accounting compliance, the following may be retained for the period required by applicable law:

  • Subscription transaction records — Apple App Store and Google Play subscription receipts, retained per Apple's and Google's terms and applicable tax law (typically up to 7 years for US tax records). These records do not contain protocol or health data.
  • Anonymized usage logs — server logs may briefly retain non-identifying technical information (request timestamps, error reports) for up to 30 days for security and operational purposes. These logs contain no personal health information.

Retained records cannot be used to reconstruct your protocol data, schedules, or any personal health information.

Cancel Your Subscription Separately

Deleting your StackMindr account does not automatically cancel your Pro subscription. App Store and Google Play subscriptions must be canceled through the platform that processed your payment:

  • Apple App Store subscriptions: Open iOS Settings → [your name] → Subscriptions → StackMindr Pro → Cancel Subscription.
  • Google Play subscriptions: Open the Google Play Store app → Profile → Payments & subscriptions → Subscriptions → StackMindr Pro → Cancel.

We recommend canceling your subscription before deleting your account if you want to stop being billed.

Recovery

Account deletion is permanent and irreversible. We cannot recover deleted data or restore deleted accounts. If you create a new StackMindr account with the same email after deletion, it will start as a fresh account with no prior history.

If you have questions about account deletion or need help with the process, contact us at justin@stackmindr.app.
